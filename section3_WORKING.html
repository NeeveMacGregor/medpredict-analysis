Key Concept: Stakeholder Analysis

Identifying all individuals and groups who will be affected by or can influence the success of MedPredict implementation.

Why It Matters:

  • Ensures all perspectives are considered in system design
  • Identifies potential resistance or support
  • Helps plan communication and training strategies
  • Prevents critical gaps in requirements gathering

üë• The Pharmacy Team's Stakeholder Analysis

1. Pharmacy Managers

Role: Decision-makers for pharmacy operations and inventory management

Interest: Reducing waste, improving efficiency, meeting budget targets

Influence: High - Final approval on workflow changes and system adoption

2. Pharmacists & Technicians

Role: Primary users who will interact with MedPredict daily

Interest: Ease of use, accuracy of predictions, integration with current workflow

Concerns: Learning curve, additional workload, trust in system recommendations

3. IT & Analytics Teams

Role: Responsible for building, implementing, and maintaining MedPredict

Interest: Technical feasibility, data quality, system integration

Requirements: Access to Epic EHR and Omnicell data, adequate resources

4. Finance Department

Role: Budget approval and ROI tracking

Interest: Cost savings, return on investment, financial risk

Expectations: Clear cost-benefit analysis, measurable outcomes
