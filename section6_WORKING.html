HIGH RISK

1. Data Quality & Accuracy Issues

Risk: Poor data quality in Epic EHR or Omnicell could lead to inaccurate forecasts, undermining trust in the system.

Mitigation Strategy:

  • Conduct 3-month data audit before development begins
  • Implement automated data validation checks
  • Create manual review process for outlier predictions
  • Phase 1 pilot allows testing with limited scope before full rollout
HIGH RISK

2. User Adoption & Resistance to Change

Risk: Pharmacy managers may not trust AI recommendations or may revert to manual methods, making the system ineffective.

Mitigation Strategy:

  • Involve pharmacy managers in system design and testing
  • Provide comprehensive training (4 hours per manager)
  • Start with "recommendation mode" where AI suggests but doesn't automate
  • Track adoption metrics and address concerns quickly
MEDIUM RISK

3. Technical Integration Challenges

Risk: Integration with Epic EHR and Omnicell may be more complex than anticipated, causing delays or cost overruns.

Mitigation Strategy:

  • IT team has prior Epic integration experience
  • Budget includes 13% contingency for integration issues
  • Phased approach allows time to resolve technical problems
  • Fallback: Use manual data exports if real-time integration fails
MEDIUM RISK

4. Model Performance Below Expectations

Risk: AI forecasting accuracy may not reach the 85% target, limiting cost savings.

Mitigation Strategy:

  • Use proven ML algorithms (Random Forest, LSTM networks)
  • Analytics team has experience with predictive modeling
  • Continuous model retraining based on new data
  • Even 70% accuracy would still improve upon current manual methods
LOW RISK

5. Project Timeline Delays

Risk: 5-month timeline may be optimistic; delays could impact ROI timeline.

Mitigation Strategy:

  • Timeline includes buffer in each phase
  • Project manager will track milestones weekly
  • Scope can be reduced if needed (fewer medication classes initially)
  • Even 6-7 month timeline still achieves ROI within year 1
